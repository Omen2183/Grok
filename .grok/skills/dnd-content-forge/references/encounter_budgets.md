# Encounter XP Budgets (5e Simplified)

Use party level and difficulty to pick a target XP budget. Mix one threatening enemy with support creatures for better pacing.

| Party Level | Easy | Medium | Hard | Deadly |
|-------------|------|--------|------|--------|
| 1 | 50 | 75 | 100 | 150 |
| 2 | 100 | 150 | 200 | 300 |
| 3 | 150 | 225 | 400 | 600 |
| 4 | 250 | 375 | 500 | 750 |
| 5 | 375 | 750 | 1100 | 1700 |
| 6 | 500 | 1000 | 1400 | 2100 |
| 7 | 600 | 1200 | 1700 | 2500 |
| 8 | 750 | 1500 | 2100 | 3200 |
| 9 | 900 | 1800 | 2600 | 3900 |
| 10 | 1100 | 2200 | 3200 | 4800 |

**Party size adjustment:** multiply budget by `(party_size / 4)`.

**CLI:** `python generate_monster.py "Campaign" --encounter --difficulty Hard`
