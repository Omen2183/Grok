# Treasure & Magic Item Guidelines

## Coin by CR (single creature defeat)
| CR | Typical GP |
|----|------------|
| 0–1 | 2d6 × 10 |
| 2–4 | 3d6 × 10 |
| 5–10 | 4d6 × 10 |
| 11–16 | 5d6 × 10 |
| 17+ | 6d6 × 10 |

## Magic item frequency (party level)
- **Levels 1–4:** uncommon consumables; rare items as major quest rewards
- **Levels 5–10:** uncommon permanent items; rare as milestone rewards
- **Levels 11–16:** rare common; very rare for bosses
- **Levels 17+:** very rare and legendary sparingly

## Ledger discipline
Always log generated items via `procedural_loot.py` so duplicates are avoided across sessions.

**CLI:** `python procedural_loot.py generate "Campaign" --cr 5`
